Who examines the title?

Patriot Title attorneys personally examine title for each transaction. This assures that the title is good and insurable. Title curative matters can be handled through the law firm of Tonry, Brinson and Glorioso.

What loans do you close?

VA, FHA, SBA, and Conventional Loans, as well as any number of non-conventional loan types, and construction loans for both residential and commercial real estate.

What happens at and after closing?

Trained, qualified staff conduct the closing. Patriot Title only accepts certified funds, so disbursement is immediate and guaranteed. After closing, Patriot Title disburses monies, prepares loan payoffs, records documents, forwards loan papers to the lender, pays liens or taxes due, updates title, issues title insurance policies, and forwards final recorded documents.

Where are your offices?

Louisiana offices are in Slidell (245 Pontchartrain Dr, Slidell, LA 70458) and Mandeville (1011 N Causeway Blvd, Mandeville, LA 70471). The Mississippi office is in Bay St. Louis. Call (985) 649-0433.

Do you offer continuing education?

Yes. Continuing education courses for real estate brokers and agents, certification courses including Notary classes, and Patriot Title is an approved vendor for continuing education for the Louisiana Real Estate Commission.
